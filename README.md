# Wind-Dispensor
I’m making an electric fan for the summer because I was tired of those manual crank ones that break your arm and suffering in silence because of the heat.

Peppermint Fan
A handheld electric fan inspired by peppermint because peppermint tastes cool, therefore it should make a cool fan.

The Idea
I got tired of manual crank fans making my hand fall off in summer heat, so I decided to make an electric one instead.

Design Process
First, I took apart an old fan and looked at random parts I had lying around, including a motor and battery from previous projects. Unfortunately, the parts didn't work, so I couldn't use them.

Then I got inspired by peppermint because it tastes cool in your mouth. This is completely scientific and definitely not a random idea.

I designed the fan in Autodesk Fusion 360 and finished the CAD model. After that, I realized I forgot to make room for the actual electronics, so I had to find the dimensions of every component and redesign a large part of the model.
